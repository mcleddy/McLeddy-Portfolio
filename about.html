About Me


Mary Leddy is a New Orleans native that relocated to Tucson in 2017. Since then she has worked in education while attending classes in web development and will complete the coding bootcamp hosted through the University of Arizona. Her focus is in CSS and Javascript for front-end work, but is also trained in comprehensive back-end work. In her spare time she also enjoys other artistic mediums, including charcoals, oil pastels, and embroidery.


She believes that web applications should be a balance of functionality and artistic expression.
